Hex-A-Gone

How to play

One of the mini games in fall guys is "Hex-A-Gone."The goal of the game is to not fall off the entire series of platforms and try to be the last one "alive." In the begining of the minigame as people run along platforms pieces of the platform breakdown so its harder to run, longer time goes by. For Hardcore Mincraft players this should sound SUPER familiar. Because it's it closley resembles Mincrafts minigame "TNT RUN." If you had played tnt run before this minigame should be really easy to play on.
